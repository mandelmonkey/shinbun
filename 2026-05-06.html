英国えいこく債さい利回りりまわり、28年ねんぶり高水準こうすいじゅんに急騰きゅうとう

英国えいこくの長期ちょうき国債こくさい利回りりまわりが1998年ねん以来いらいの高水準こうすいじゅんに達したっした。30年ねん物もの国債こくさいは約やく5.78%に上昇じょうしょうし、10年ねん物ものも大幅おおはばに高騰こうとうしている。ホルムズ海峡かいきょう危機ききに伴うともなうエネルギー価格かかくの上昇じょうしょうに加えくわえ、イングランド・スコットランド・ウェールズでの選挙せんきょを控えひかえた政治的せいじてき不確実性ふかくじつせいが市場しじょうを圧迫あっぱくしている。利回りりまわりの上昇じょうしょうにより、リーブス財務ざいむ相そうの財政ざいせい規律きりつの維持いじはさらに困難こんなんとなる見通しみとおしである。

UK bond yields surge to 28-year high

Long-dated UK government bond yields have climbed to their highest levels since 1998, with 30-year gilts reaching around 5.78%. Markets are reacting to higher global energy prices linked to the Strait of Hormuz crisis and political uncertainty ahead of elections across England, Scotland and Wales. The rise puts additional pressure on Chancellor Reeves' fiscal rules.

UK election campaign enters final sprint

More than 5,000 council seats in England, six mayoralties, and devolved elections in Scotland and Wales are at stake as parties make their final appeals. With both Labour and the Conservatives on the defensive, Reform UK, the Liberal Democrats and the Greens all hope to turn public frustration into gains. The results will offer a key snapshot of political fragmentation in Britain.

Oil prices dip as US pauses Hormuz escort operation for Iran talks

Oil prices fell modestly after President Trump announced a pause in the US naval escort operation in the Strait of Hormuz to test whether a broader deal with Iran can be reached. The strait carries roughly a fifth of global oil and gas shipments. Analysts warn there is still little concrete evidence that shipping through the waterway will fully normalise soon.

Apple pays $250m to settle AI features lawsuit

Golden Week ends with nationwide return-travel congestion
