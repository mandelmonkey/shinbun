💡 The Secret of Pop Music
About 90% of pop songs use just 4 chords. In the key of C major, that's:
C
G
Am
F
The pattern: I – V – vi – IV. Learn this in every key and you're halfway there.
🎵 Why 7th chords?
7th chords add colour and sophistication. The V chord is always a dominant 7th (G7) — that tension wants to resolve home to the I. The I and IV are major 7ths. The ii, iii, vi are minor 7ths. Learn these and your playing will sound full and musical.
